Interactive Media Arts @ NYU

For the class Design Fundamentals, I designed a logo for NYU IMA program, an Interactive Media Arts undergraduate program at New York University. I also designed a poster for the 2021 IMA Winter Showcase, and developed a wayfinding system for the 4th floor ITP/IMA building, where the ITP/IMA program is located at.
