Citation

UI/UX Design & Research Intern @ Hall Pass Studios

For the summer of 2021, I interned at Hall Pass Studios. I worked on a project Citation - a web adventure game that is designed to incentivize and engage NYU students to learn academic integrity concepts. I first worked on creating art and designing game mechanics. Once the alpha version was ready, I was responsible for conducting play-testing and making iterations based on the players' behaviors and feedbacks.
