Hello! I am Soojin Lee and I am an interactive designer/developer studying Interactive Media at New York University Abu Dhabi. I see the world through a lens of optimism, and provide technology-powered design solutions to complex problems.

I was born in Korea, grew up in China, and currently based in New York and Abu Dhabi. My blended background has shaped me to truly care for the community that I join, and be comfortable working for people with diverse needs.

My goal is to find:

the people that I need me the most
the business that needs me the most
the project that needs me the most

because it is really meaningful for me to spend my time and energy in making the use of the opportunities, privileges, talents, and perspectives I have to the best potential.
