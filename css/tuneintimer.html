  Tune-in-Timer

Mobile App UI/UX Design

Timer meets Spotify! Innovated and designed UI/UX for Tune-in Timer, a timer application with a music streaming function.

Have you got 15 minutes to get ready? Do you listen to music when you take shower? Listen to your favourite songs while stay track on time with Tune-in-Timer, a mobile timer application that countdown time with music. It is a perfect app to enhance productivity, elevate mood, and bring music to your daily life! Set time, select playlist and start tuning in to every second of your life!

Virtual Fridge

Users can view items in the fridge at one glance. Items are displayed in categories. Users can customize category by pressing Add category + on the top right corner. Each item is displayed with a color sticker that indicates the freshness of the food; ● (good), ● (okay), and ● (Risky).

Customize Enter

Users can customize enter for the items that are not on the list, such as left-over food, by taking photos.

Today's Dish

Check recommended recipes with items available in your fridge. Select items you wish to use and get a recipe recommendation. There is also an alaert bar that notifies users with the food that should be consumed soon.

Recipe

Based on the entry and available ingredients, fridger recommends users with easy, nutirious, and delicious recipes. Click heart button to add the dish to your favorites, or click share button to share the recipe with friends.

Cooking & Update

When finished with cooking, Fridger displays a notification that reminds users to update virtual fridge. It displays a list of ingredients used for the recipe. Click all the ingredients they used, and update the fridge.

Grocery

Make grocery shopping list. This will be connected to the third-party online grocery apps.

Favorites & Share

Bookmark recipes and share with friends. By clicking sharing button on the top right corner of each food image, users can share the recipe with friends.
