  Lifegram

Future Social Media UI/UX Design

Lifegram is the future social marketplace where people can stay connected with people and the surroundings with an integrated AR/VR technology.

Capture Immersive Experiences

Beyond 2D videos and audios, capture immersive experiences using "Experience" feature on Lifegram. Collect 360 view, the sound, the smell, and the feeling around you. Share your experience with your friends or save to experience later.

Customise Sensorial Experiences

Users can selectively choose what sensorial experiences to capture or to share. Customise the content by turning on/off visual, audio, or the olfaction.

Share Experience

Instead of taking "video" or a "picture" to share moments of your life, in Lifegram, you can capture the immersive experience that includes 360 view, temperature, humidity, sound, smell, and the feelings of the environment.

Realtime Information

Stay informed and get timely feedback. Search for a real-time questions like "What is the best selling beverage in this cafe?". Users will receive a timely information directly from the cafe.

Lifegram AR Shopping

See the world with the commercial information digital overlay. Check out the price, the reviews, and directly communicate with the owner for non-commercial items.

AR/VR Try-on

Try-on before you buy. Using AR/VR Try-on technology, wear clothes digitally to see how it fits on you or place furnitures digitally in hour house.

Metaverse Education

Moving beyond Zoom classroom, join a class at a virtual space where you enter the classroom as an avatar and your body movements are fully embodied by your avatars.
