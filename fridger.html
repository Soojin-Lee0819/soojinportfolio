  Fridger

Mobile App UI/UX Design

Innovated and designed UI/UX for Fridger, all-in-one fridge management app. Reduce food waste and make your cooking routine easier with Fridger!

Stop throwing away food. Make delicious meal with ingredients already available at home. Fridger, your ultimate kitchen assistance, is here to help you to manage your fridge, do grocery shopping, plan meal, and reduce food waste.

Scan to Start Virtual Fridge

Virtual Fridge

View items in the fridge at your finter-tip. Items are displayed in categories and can be customized by pressing Add category + . Color stickers indicate the freshness of the food; ● (good), ● (okay), and ● (Risky).

Manage Items

View, add, or edit items in the fridge by clicking + button. Track its freshness, quantity, and make notes. If the item is short in supply, click + Grocery List button to add to your grocery list.

Customize Enter

Users can customize enter for the items that are not on the list (e.g. left-over food), by taking photos.

Today's Dish

Get recipe recommendation with items available in your fridge. Select items you wish to use. There is also an alaert bar that notifies users with the food that should be consumed soon.

Recipe

Based on the entry and available ingredients, fridger recommends users with easy, nutirious, and delicious recipes. Add the dish to your favorites, or share with friends.

Cooking & Update

When finished with cooking, Fridger displays a notification that reminds users to update virtual fridge. It displays the list of ingredients used for the recipe. Click all the ingredients they used, and update the fridge.

Grocery

Make grocery shopping list. This will be connected to the third-party online grocery apps.

Favorites & Share

Bookmark recipes to favourites and share with friends.
